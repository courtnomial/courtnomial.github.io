I sing and play keyboards (piano, organ, harpsichord). I studied classical voice and historical keyboard at the MUK in Vienna, Austria for a semester abroad. I also attended the Brevard Summer Jazz Institute for jazz voice. During my time at Duke, I familiarized myself with every facet of the music department, including performance, musicology, composition, and technology.

I'm currently recording my first EP called Sweeter Grapes. It's inspired by the vulnerability of jazz, the freeness of southern rock, and the experimentation of noise.
